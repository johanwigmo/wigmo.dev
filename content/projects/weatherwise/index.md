WeatherWise helps outdoor enthusiasts plan their activities based on accurate, location-specific weather data.

## Features

- Activity-specific weather recommendations
- Hourly and 10-day forecasts
- Severe weather alerts
- Customizable widgets

## Tech Stack

- **Language:** Kotlin
- **UI:** Jetpack Compose
- **Platform:** Android
