I'm excited to announce the launch of WeatherWise, a weather app built specifically for outdoor enthusiasts.

WeatherWise helps you plan outdoor activities by providing activity-specific weather recommendations. Whether you're planning a hike, a bike ride, or a round of disc golf, WeatherWise gives you the information you need.

Available now on the Google Play Store.
